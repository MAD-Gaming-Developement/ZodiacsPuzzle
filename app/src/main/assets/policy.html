Zodiacs Puzzle: DATA AND PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 15, 2024

Introduction

This Data Policy outlines how your data is utilized by the Zodiacs Puzzle app. Our commitment is to safeguard your privacy and adhere to all relevant data protection laws and regulations.

Age Restriction

Zodiac Puzzle is designed for users aged 18 and above. Individuals below 17 years old are advised against accessing the app due to potential content unsuitable for younger audiences.

Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data from you and rest assured that this is not shared to any third-party apps:
Device Data : Information about the device used to access our Services, such as the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
Coarse Location: We might gather your approximate location to offer you location-related functionalities or advertisements.
Post Notifications: We may seek your authorization to send notifications, ensuring you stay informed about updates, events, and promotions.
AD_ID: We might utilize the advertising ID supplied by your device to customize the advertisements displayed within the App.
Network State and Internet: We may gather details about your network connection and internet usage to enhance the performance of the App and facilitate troubleshooting.
AppsFlyer Data: Specific in-app occurrences (such as accepting/declining policies, accessing settings, navigating the main menu, playing, and exiting) are examples of custom events. Install and uninstall events are automatically gathered by AppsFlyer.
Gameplay Data: We gather information regarding your gameplay, including scores, earned points, and progress in the game. This data is utilized to personalize your experience and enhance the performance of the App.
Data Analytics: Zodiac Puzzle collects Personal Information necessary for its functions, primarily obtained directly from individuals. In certain instances, information may be sourced from third-party entities.

Data Use

We use your data for the following purposes:
App functionality: To provide and operate the App's features and services.
Communication: To send you important information about the App, such as updates, bug fixes, and special offers.
Analytics: To improve the App by understanding how you and other users interact with it.
Advertising: To personalize and display advertising within the App.

Data Security

We employ reasonable safeguards to ensure the security of your data, preventing unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Utilizing secure servers and data encryption, we protect your data both during transmission and when stored.

Data Retention

We will retain your data for the following periods: Device data and gameplay data will be retained for 90 days after you last use the App. Location data and network data will be retained for 24 hours. AD_ID data will be retained in accordance with Google's Play Store policies.

User Data Deletion

You can request to delete your data by contacting us at developer@vapourcore.site. We will delete your data within a reasonable timeframe, subject to any legal or regulatory requirements.

The App requests the following permissions:

Post Notifications: To send you important information about the App (optional).
AD_ID: To personalize advertising you see in the App.

You can control which permissions you grant the App at any time through your device settings.

No User Accounts

The App does not create user accounts. We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
